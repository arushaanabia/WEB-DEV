CLASSIC PANCAKESü•û

Pancakes are a delicious breakfast dish, loved worldwide. Soft, fluffy, and easy to prepare, they can be enjoyed with fruits, syrup, or even a sprinkle of powdered sugar. Perfect for both kids and adults.

This recipe takes only 20 minutes and serves about 4 people. Lets get started with the ingredients and instructions below.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¬Ω tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg, buttermilk, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients; mix gently until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan, grease lightly, and pour ¬º cup batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with syrup, fruits, or butter!
